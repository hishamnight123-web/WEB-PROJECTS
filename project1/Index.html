About Our College

Welcome to Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad

The Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad was established in the year 1980 under the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Kerala, with the noble vision of empowering the people by offering quality technical education. The polytechnic college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and offers six diploma programs in engineering and technology.

The campus is located at Kodumbu village in Palakkad Taluk, Palakkad district, along National Highway 544, and is spread across 33 acres of panoramic greenery. Coimbatore International Airport is around 60 km away from the college. With over thirty-eight years of exemplary performance, our college has established itself as a centre of excellence and is one of the most reputed institutions in Kerala.

Apart from the regular courses, evening diploma courses and vocational courses are also offered here.
